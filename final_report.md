## Summary
Recipes Worldwide is a recipe sharing platform that emphasizes discovering the cuisine of other cultures. After creating an account, the website allows users to create and edit their own recipes, viewing and rate others' recipes, and explore an interactive map of the world to see popular dishes from each country.
